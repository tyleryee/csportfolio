Golf

Golf is an amazing sport that was started in Scottland. Over many years, the sport migrated to America where it became a sport for the rich. The sport is great because it test how good you are as a player independently. People all over the world play in tournaments to test their skill against other players and to win money.

Boy Scouts

Boy Scouts was started in 1910 by Robert Baden-Powell. The purpose of Boy Scouts was to train boys how to be the best and to prepare them for adulthood. Boy Scouts is broken into troops across the nation where boys are affiliated to. National Jamborees happen every 4 years where most meet other people and where the current president is invited to speak.
